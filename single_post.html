Residential proxies are IP addresses provided by Internet Service Providers (ISPs) to homeowners. Unlike datacenter proxies, residential proxies are associated with actual physical locations, making them appear as legitimate users to websites.

Key Benefits of Residential Proxies

  • Better anonymity and reduced block rates
  • Access geo-restricted content
  • More reliable scraping at scale
